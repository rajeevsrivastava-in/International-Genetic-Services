About us

IGS was founded by a group of clinicians and scientists who recognized a gap in the way genetic testing is currently being offered outside of the United States. Our mission is to provide patients around the world access to genetic testing and the cutting edge technology necessary to perform this testing. We are dedicated to providing friendly, knowledgeable, and consistent customer service to our clients regardless of where they reside. Through our relationships with U.S vendors, we are able to partner with foreign clients in order to offer a wide range of tests at reasonable prices. We are at a point where genetic testing is instrumental in formulation of treatment plans, guiding surgical planning, and most importantly, positively impacting clinical outcomes. We strongly feel that this technology should not be limited to the U.S and to only those who can afford it.


our perspective

Recent estimates place this industry as one of the fastest growing sectors in the US, with very little presence in other countries, specifically in Latin America. Whether being used to predict one’s susceptibility to developing a hereditary disease or to see how one may metabolize a specific drug, genetic testing is fast becoming a standard of care. Many clinicians in other countries need access to this testing, but up to now, options are limited. Very few foreign labs are capable of performing much of the testing to the necessary standards, further limiting access. U.S based laboratories have a strong desire to be able to offer testing in these markets, but do not have an international sales force or know-how to make inroads into these markets with different healthcare systems and pricing. In addition, there is little incentive to leave the U.S as business is booming, leading to high prices.
