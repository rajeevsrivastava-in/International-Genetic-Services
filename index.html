About us

our perspective

IGS ( International Genetic Services) is a US based company in Houston, Texas.

With a comprehensive team comprising of clinicians and scientists, our mission is to bring genetic testing to the forefront of medicine,
and offer cutting-edge and personalized care to individuals around the world.

Executives:

Thinh Phan, MD

CEO and Co-founder
